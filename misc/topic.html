Chosen Technology Topic:

Recommendation Algorithms in the Digital World and

their Effects on Society & Adolescents

This topic regards the use of recommendation algorithms within frequently used digital systems such as social media platforms, online retail websites, businesses' marketing schemes and advertisements, which tailor content and experiences to individuals based on their actions, patterns, behaviours and interactions.

This is an extremely significant topic to cover in today's day and age, where the majority of the systems society interacts with are intergrated with such algorithms, serving to provide more affable and efficient experiences to users globally. Adolescents in particular are impacted by these recommendation algorithms, as they interact with them the most on a daily basis through all of the digital systems mentioned previously. By doing so, these algorithms are able to affect and even dictate a wide range of things including (but not limited to) day-to-day lives, habits, beliefs, mindsets, emotions, actions, decision-making, judgement, privacy and knowledge. These affected areas within people, and particularly adolescents, are imperative to acknowledge and examine, as influencing them via stated algorithms can bring both good and bad consequences for society as a whole.

Thus, the research within this website intends to highlight the importance of recommendation algorithms and their frequently utilised recommendation, personalisation and tailoring capabilities. It explores the many societal opportunities, risks and choices that are brought forth through the integration of recommendation algorithms within popular digital systems, aiming to provide conclusive information and awareness as to how society can navigate today's digital world with these algorithms in mind.
