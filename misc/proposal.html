Please briefly describe your topic, its relevance and importance, and why you have chosen it.
Topic Description Description and why it is important
Topic-description This topic regards the use of algorithms within frequently used digital systems such as social media platforms, online retail websites, businesses' marketing schemes and advertisements, which tailor content and experiences to individuals based on their actions, patterns, behaviours and interactions. This is an extremely significant topic to cover because, in today's day and age, the majority of the systems society interacts with are intergrated with algorithms, serving to provide more affable and efficient experiences to users globally. Adolescents in particular are impacted by these algorithms, as they interact with them the most on a daily basis through all of the digital systems mentioned previously. The importance of algorithmic personalisation extends beyond the effects it has on adolescents as individuals, and thus, our topic aims to cover the effects and implications on society as a whole, as well as adolescents. It is imperative to acknowledge that the societal effects these algorithms have on people are serious and drastic, as they affect: day-to-day lives, habits, beliefs, mindsets, emotions, actions, decision-making, judgement, privacy and knowledge, to name a few crucial areas.
